Delicioso Alfredo Recipe!

Learn how to make alfredo quick and easy!.

Alfredo is an Italian pasta that mostly utilizes parmesan cheese in its mix along with butter and heavy cream to make a sauce that coats the pasta! Now, let's get started!

First things first! You'll need to gather some ingredients!

    Ingredients

  • ¼ Teaspoon Pepper
  • ½ Teaspoon Italian Seasoning
  • ½ Teaspoon Salt
  • ½ Cup Butter
  • ½ 1 ½ Cups Heavy Whipping Cream
  • ½ 2 Teaspoons Garlic Minced
  • ½ C Cups Freshly Grated Parmesan Cheese

Prep Instructions

  1. Add the butter and cream to a large skillet.
  2. Simmer over low heat for 2 minutes.
  3. Whisk in the garlic, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper for one minute.
  4. Whisk in the parmesan cheese until melted.
  5. Serve and watch your taste buds melt!
